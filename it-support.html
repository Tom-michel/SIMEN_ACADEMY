IT Support Bootcamp

Description

The training bootcamp is aimed at individuals or IT professionals seeking to enter the industry. This training focuses on the practical aspects of technical support jobs.

You will improve your skills in supporting, installing, configuring, and maintaining Operating Systems. The bootcamp will cover:

Windows services, Active Directory management, operating system installations, web servers, remote access services and performance monitoring.

Duration

  • 16 weeks
  • One Lecture session of two hours every weeks
  • One office hour session of one hour every week

Prerequisites

  • Basic Computer Skills
